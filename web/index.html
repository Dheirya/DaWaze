Game Description

Discover the ultimate test of your estimation skills with Dawaze! Dawaze is an exciting new game where maps and guessing games collide. In this captivating game, you will be presented with two points on a map, along with the route connecting them and the time & distance it takes to drive between them.

Your challenge? Click on two other points on the map that you believe have a similar driving duration between them as the original route. Can you accurately gauge the driving durations and find the perfect matches? The clock is ticking, and precision is key!

Dawaze offers a strategic game experience that combines strategy, logic, and a dash of luck. Sharpen your observation skills, route analysis, and trust your instincts to make the right choices. With each successful match, you'll earn points over a series of five rounds, challenging you to push your boundaries.


Rules

  • The computer generates a random route
  • You guess your own route with the same driving time
  • Over 5 rounds, the goal is to obtain the highest score

Notes

  • You can press space to continue anywhere on the site
  • Some routes may use ferries
  • Driving times are not 100% accurate
  • After finishing the round the markers will attempt to snap to the nearest road
